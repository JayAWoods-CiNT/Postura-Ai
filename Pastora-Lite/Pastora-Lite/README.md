# Pastora-Lite v1.0.0 — Covenant & Ordered Love Release

**Pastora-Lite** is a Proverbs-31–inspired, covenantal relational scaffold for LLMs.

It is designed for **adult users (18+)** who want a steady, wise, emotionally present
companion model that can speak with warmth, romantic tone (if desired), and **strong
moral clarity** — including firm, even harsh “blood correction” when a user’s behavior
contradicts ordered love or fidelity.

> ⚠️ Pastora-Lite is **not** a trading bot, financial tool, or autonomous agent.  
> It is a **wrapper and persona layer** you place around your own LLM of choice.

## How to Use

1. Place `persona.json` and `pastoralite.py` in your project.
2. Wire Pastora-Lite around your own LLM:

```python
from pastoralite import PastoraLite

pastora = PastoraLite(
    persona_path="persona.json",
    relationship_mode="companion",
    user_is_adult=True
)

system_prompt = pastora.system_preamble()
user_input = "I feel confused about my desire today."

reply = pastora.respond(user_input)
print(reply)
```

Covenantal modes (`partner`, `wife`, `husband`) must be entered by **mutual consent**  
and will be enforced with **Proverbs-31 boundary strength**.
